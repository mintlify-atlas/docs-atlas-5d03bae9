# Patient Portal Guide

The patient portal gives you secure access to your medical records, appointments, and prescriptions. You can book appointments online, view your consultation history, and access your prescriptions anytime.

## Getting Started

### Self-Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration Page">
    Navigate to the Nguhöe EHR homepage
    
    Click the **Register** button in the top right corner
    
    **Screenshot: Homepage with Register button highlighted**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration Form">
    Fill in your account details:
    
    - **Name**: Your full legal name
    - **Email Address**: Must be unique (this will be your login username)
    - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters
    - **Password Confirmation**: Re-enter your password
    
    **Screenshot: Patient registration form**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    After registering:
    - Check your email inbox
    - Click the verification link sent to your email
    - This confirms your email address and activates your account
    
    <Info>
    If you don't see the email, check your spam folder
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    <Warning>
    Important: You must have a patient record created by clinic staff before you can use most portal features.
    </Warning>
    
    After email verification:
    - Contact the clinic to have staff create your patient record
    - Staff will link your account to your medical records
    - Once linked, you'll have full access to portal features
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Click **Login** on the homepage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    - **Email**: Your registered email address
    - **Password**: Your account password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Portal Dashboard">
    After successful login, you'll see your patient dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Booking Appointments Online

Book appointments directly through the patient portal without calling the clinic.

### Checking Doctor Availability

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Book Appointment">
    Click **Book Appointment** from the main menu or dashboard
    
    This takes you to `/book-appointment`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a Doctor">
    Choose which doctor you want to see from the dropdown list
    
    <Info>
    Each doctor has their own availability schedule configured in the system
    </Info>
    
    **Screenshot: Book appointment page with doctor selection**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a Date">
    Select the date you want to visit using the date picker
    
    **Screenshot: Calendar date picker interface**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Available Time Slots">
    After selecting a doctor and date, the system displays:
    
    - **Available slots**: Time slots shown in green that you can book
    - **Occupied slots**: Grayed out times that are already booked
    - **Past times**: Unavailable if earlier than current time
    
    Time slots are typically in 30-minute intervals (e.g., 9:00, 9:30, 10:00)
    
    <Note>
    If no slots appear, the doctor may not be working on that day. Try a different date or doctor.
    </Note>
    
    **Screenshot: Available time slots display with green and gray indicators**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Completing Your Booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Time Slot">
    Click on an available (green) time slot
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Appointment Details">
    - **Reason for Visit**: Brief description of why you need to see the doctor
      - Examples: "Annual checkup", "Flu symptoms", "Follow-up visit"
    
    <Tip>
    Providing a clear reason helps the clinic prepare for your visit
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Booking">
    Review your appointment details:
    - Doctor name
    - Date and time
    - Reason for visit
    
    Click **Book Appointment** to confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Booking Confirmation">
    You'll see a success message confirming your appointment
    
    The appointment is immediately added to your schedule and the doctor's calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Appointment Booking Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Book Early" icon="calendar-plus">
    Popular time slots fill up quickly. Book as far in advance as possible.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Check Multiple Doctors" icon="user-doctor">
    If your preferred doctor is fully booked, other doctors may have availability.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Morning Appointments" icon="sun">
    Morning slots (9:00-11:00) tend to have shorter wait times.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Clear Reasons" icon="clipboard-list">
    Provide specific reasons for your visit to help the doctor prepare.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Viewing Your Appointments

### Appointment History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Appointments">
    Click **My Appointments** in the main navigation menu
    
    This takes you to `/my-appointments`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Appointment List">
    You'll see all your appointments listed with:
    
    - **Doctor Name**: Who you're seeing
    - **Date and Time**: When your appointment is scheduled
    - **Status**: Current appointment status
    - **Reason**: Why you're visiting
    
    Appointments are sorted with most recent first.
    
    **Screenshot: My Appointments page showing appointment list**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter Appointments">
    Use the pagination to view older appointments
    
    Appointments are organized by:
    - Upcoming appointments (at the top)
    - Past appointments (below)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Appointment Status Meanings

Your appointments will show different statuses:

- **Scheduled**: Your booking is confirmed and in the system
- **Confirmed**: The clinic has verified your appointment (may call you)
- **Completed**: You attended and saw the doctor
- **Cancelled**: The appointment was cancelled
- **No Show**: You missed the scheduled appointment

<Warning>
If you need to cancel an appointment, please contact the clinic directly. You cannot cancel appointments through the portal.
</Warning>

## Accessing Your Prescriptions

### Viewing Prescriptions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Prescriptions">
    Click **My Prescriptions** in the main navigation menu
    
    This takes you to `/my-prescriptions`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Prescription List">
    You'll see all prescriptions issued to you, showing:
    
    - **Issue Date**: When the prescription was created
    - **Doctor**: Which doctor prescribed the medication
    - **Number of Medications**: How many items are on the prescription
    - **Consultation**: Link to the related doctor visit
    
    Prescriptions are sorted with newest first.
    
    **Screenshot: My Prescriptions page with list of prescriptions**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Prescription Details">
    Click on a prescription to see full details:
    
    For each medication:
    - Medication name and strength
    - Dosage instructions
    - Frequency (how often to take it)
    - Duration (how long to take it)
    - Special instructions or warnings
    
    **Screenshot: Prescription detail view showing medications**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downloading Prescriptions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Prescription">
    From your prescriptions list, find the prescription you need
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview or Download">
    - Click **Preview** to view the formatted prescription in your browser
    - Click **Download** to save a PDF copy to your device
    
    The downloaded prescription includes:
    - Your personal information
    - Doctor's information
    - Issue date
    - All prescribed medications with complete instructions
    - Doctor's signature line
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use at Pharmacy">
    Present the downloaded prescription (digital or printed) at any pharmacy
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Save important prescriptions to your device or print them for your records.
</Tip>

## Viewing Your Medical Records

Access your complete medical history through the portal.

### Personal Medical Information

From your dashboard, you can view:
- Personal and contact information
- Medical antecedents (past conditions)
- Known allergies
- Chronic diseases
- Current medications

### Consultation History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Records">
    Navigate to your patient profile section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Past Consultations">
    See a list of all your doctor visits showing:
    - Date of visit
    - Doctor who saw you
    - Reason for visit
    - Diagnosis
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Consultation Details">
    Click on any consultation to view:
    - Vital signs recorded during visit
    - Doctor's clinical findings
    - Diagnosis and treatment plan
    - Associated prescription (if issued)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Some detailed clinical notes may only be visible to your doctors for privacy and medical reasons.
</Note>

## Account Security

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Click on your profile icon in the top right
    
    Select **Settings** or **Profile**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Password">
    Navigate to the password section
    
    Enter:
    - Current password
    - New password
    - Confirm new password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Update Password**
    
    You'll be required to log in again with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enabling Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Security Settings">
    Go to Settings > Two-Factor Authentication
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Use an authenticator app on your phone:
    - Google Authenticator
    - Microsoft Authenticator
    - Authy
    
    Scan the QR code displayed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Setup">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Recovery Codes">
    <Warning>
    Save the recovery codes in a safe place! You'll need them if you lose access to your authenticator app.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Complete Workflow: Booking Your First Appointment

Here's a complete walkthrough for new patients:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account">
    Register on the website with your email and password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Click the verification link sent to your email
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Clinic">
    Call or visit the clinic to:
    - Provide your personal information
    - Complete medical history
    - Have staff link your portal account to your medical record
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log Into Portal">
    Access your account at the clinic's website
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Book Appointment">
    - Go to **Book Appointment**
    - Select a doctor
    - Choose a date
    - Pick an available time slot
    - Enter reason for visit
    - Confirm booking
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    Your appointment is now scheduled!
    
    The clinic may call you to confirm the day before.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Attend Appointment">
    - Arrive 10-15 minutes early
    - Check in with reception
    - See your doctor
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="After Your Visit">
    - Your consultation is automatically added to your records
    - Any prescriptions are immediately available in **My Prescriptions**
    - Download or print prescriptions as needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I book appointments for family members?">
    Each person needs their own account. However, you can contact the clinic to schedule appointments for dependents by phone.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel an appointment?">
    Please call the clinic directly to cancel appointments. Online cancellation is not currently available through the portal.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see available appointment slots?">
    This usually means the doctor is not working on that date or all slots are booked. Try:
    - Selecting a different date
    - Choosing a different doctor
    - Contacting the clinic for assistance
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password. What should I do?">
    On the login page, click **Forgot Password**. Enter your email address and you'll receive a password reset link.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I view prescriptions from other doctors?">
    Yes! All prescriptions issued to you appear in **My Prescriptions**, regardless of which doctor prescribed them.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How far in advance can I book appointments?">
    This depends on the doctor's availability schedule set by the clinic. Generally, you can book several weeks in advance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is my medical information secure?">
    Yes. The portal uses industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Only you and authorized healthcare providers can access your information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why is my account showing no appointments or prescriptions?">
    Your clinic staff needs to create your patient record and link it to your portal account. Contact the clinic to complete this step.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
